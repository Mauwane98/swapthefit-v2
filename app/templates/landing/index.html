The Yearly Uniform Headache.

Every year, parents face the same problems with school uniforms. It's time for a better way.

  • It's Expensive: Buying new uniforms every year costs a lot of money.
  • It's Wasteful: Good uniforms that kids outgrow often end up in the trash.
  • It's Hard: Finding the right size and school item on other sites is frustrating.

SwapTheFit is the Simple Fix.

We connect parents in the same school community to make things easy.

  • Save Money: Get affordable uniforms from other parents at your school.
  • Help The Planet: Give perfectly good uniforms a new life and reduce waste.
  • Find Easily: Search by school, size, and item to get exactly what you need.
